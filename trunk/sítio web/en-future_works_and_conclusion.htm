Future Works and Conclusion

Future Works

Over the work developed, new functionalities will be implemented in the future, from which we can outstand the following:

  • Functionalities that allow to undo, redo, cut, copy and paste;
  • An application to manage the visual controls that exist in the application;
  • Possibility to add events to controls implemented in the draw area;
  • Creation of several visual interfaces, simultaneously;
  • Discreet grid over the draw area, to help the user arranging the interface.


Conclusion

This project allowed above all a deepening of knowledge at the level of applications that make the development of graphical interfaces easier. It also allowed the application of knowledge previously acquired in various classes of the course.

In short, this project was extremely competitive and interesting due to the diverse knowledge that it required being that it will be equally interesting to follow the evolution process of the developed application, as well as, the increase of its potentialities and functionalities.
